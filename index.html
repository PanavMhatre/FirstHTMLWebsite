Here are some interesting facts:

  • Spain has won 4 Euros making it the country with the most Euro trophies
  • England is the only team to lose twice, back to back in a Euros final
  • Rodri becomes the UEFA Player of the tournament
  • Harry Kane wins the Golden Boot
  • Spain's coach, Luis Enrique, becomes the first coach to win the Euros as a player and a coach
  • Spain's goalkeeper, Unai Simon, wins the Golden Glove
  • Lamine Yamal becomes the youngest player in Euros to score
  • Dani Olmo scores the winning goal!
